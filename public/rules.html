Feedback rules

In order to make the Dragonfly Client and all of its components even better, we need your help. So if you have a bug in the client, on the website or on our Discord server, please do not hesitate to report it here.

In addition, you should always pay attention to your choice of words, as it could be hurtful or even disturbing to other people.
We reserve the right to delete all posts that violate one of the above points. Threats or the like are no trivial offenses and are reported immediately.

However, before you start writing, you should first take a look at the rules and guidelines of this platform.

General

  1. Before creating a new topic, please read the FAQ and make sure you don't repeat what has already been posted. It is possible that what you want to know has already been discussed. This will save you and other members a lot of time.
  2. Please try to write grammatically correctly. Review your post before creating it.
  3. Private correspondence in public areas is considered an off topic. Please use private messages for this.
  4. Please do not copy the content of other pages into your post. If there is no way around it, format it as a quote and reference a source. (link URL)

  3. You acknowledge that the operator assumes no responsibility for the content of contributions that he has not created himself or that he has taken note of. You allow the operator to delete or block your user account, contributions and functions at any time.
  4. You also allow the operator to change your contributions if they contradict the above. Violate rules or are capable of causing damage to the operator or a third party.

Not recommended

  1. The creation of signatures in another language. Other members have the right to understand the written text. Write your post in the language that you previously specified. Exceptions are, for example, technical terms. The administration reserves the right to delete these signatures. General

Forbidden

  1. The use of swear words and / or rough expressions (also not in obscure form)

  4. Creating posts in capital letters (EXAMPLE) or alternating upper and lower case letters (eXAmPle), excessive use of bold, italic or underlined text, as well as font size.
